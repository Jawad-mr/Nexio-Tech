The platform

One foundation. Six products running on it.

Every Nexio Tech product shares the same core — fast on low-end devices, works offline where it matters, and easy enough that a new staff member learns it in an afternoon.

Modular by design

Install one product or all six. Each runs independently — no bloated all-in-one suite forcing features you don't need.

Fast on any hardware

Built to run smoothly on the mid-range tablets and desktops businesses already own — no forced upgrades.

Your data stays yours

Sales, member, and guest data lives on your device — not locked behind a subscription you might one day stop paying.

Set up in days

No procurement cycle. Message us, we configure it for your business, and it's running by the end of the week.

How it's built

Under the hood.

The technical decisions behind Nexio Tech are made for shop floors and front desks, not for engineering demos.

01 · INTERFACE

Built for touch and speed

Every screen is designed for a counter or a front desk — large tap targets, minimal typing, and a layout that survives a busy Saturday.

02 · RELIABILITY

Works when the internet doesn't
