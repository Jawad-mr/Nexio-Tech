About Nexio Tech

Software built by watching real shop floors, not spreadsheets.

Nexio Tech is the SaaS and technology venture of JSN CREATIVE — built to give small and mid-sized businesses the same operational tooling that large chains take for granted.

The starting point

It started with one bakery.

Before Nexio Tech existed as a product line, JSN CREATIVE built a point-of-sale system for a single bakery client — handling GST calculation, stock logging, and receipt sharing over WhatsApp. It worked well enough that the same thinking got applied to gyms, restaurants, and hotels.

Each product since has followed the same rule: start from how the business actually runs day to day, not from a generic template stretched to fit.

Today Nexio Tech runs six products, all built and supported directly by the founding team — no outsourced support desk, no disappearing after the sale.

What we hold to

The principles behind every product.

Built for the specific, not the generic

A bakery and a hotel don't run the same way. Neither should their software.

Ownership over rental

Pay once, own it. Software that quietly meters your business every month isn't the model we build on.

Support from a real person

The team that built the product answers the WhatsApp message — not a rotating support queue.

Simple enough for day one

If new staff can't learn it in an afternoon, it's not ready to ship.

Part of something larger

Nexio Tech is one venture under JSN CREATIVE.

JSN CREATIVE is a multi-arm studio spanning education, freelancing, and technology. Nexio Tech is its SaaS and technology arm — sitting alongside ventures in education and design.
